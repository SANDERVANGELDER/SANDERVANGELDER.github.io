My name is Sander Van Gelder and I'm a painter who lives and studies in Ghent. My main goal in life is to travel the globe in search of the most incredible landscapes in order to paint them and make them visible to the rest of the world. If people don't have the guts to travel to foreign lands  far away, I will do it for them. There lies no greater pleasure in life than in travel.
