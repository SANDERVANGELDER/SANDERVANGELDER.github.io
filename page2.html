Process of work

September 2017

I visited Ecuador for 1 month in September. I realised that painting landscapes would become my newest subject in my soon to be made paintings. I saw one of the largest peaks in the world, I lived in the Amazon for 4 days. Everything was clear to me and I couldn't wait much longer to start painting again when I was home. 

October 2017

I started to find new ways of expressing the scenery I witnessed in South America. I began experimenting with new media and soon was satisfied of what I made.
